#### 2. Inefficient Workflows

#### 3. Collaboration Barriers

- Remote editing impossible

- Time zone delays

#### 4. Rising Costs
- $5M annual storage expenses
- Growing 40% year-over-year
- Redundant software licenses
- Overtime labor costs

#### 5. Security Vulnerabilities
- Uncontrolled asset distribution
- No audit trails
- GDPR compliance risks
- Potential IP theft
