## Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Tips
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use emojis sparingly
- Create urgency/curiosity
- Personalize when possible
- A/B test variations

### Design Guidelines
- Mobile-responsive
- Clear CTAs
- Scannable format
- Brand consistency
- Alt text for images

### Segmentation Strategy
- Industry vertical
- Company size
- User role
- Engagement level
- Feature usage

### Metrics to Track
- Open rate (target: 25%+)
- Click rate (target: 7%+)
- Conversion rate
- Unsubscribe rate
- Forward rate
